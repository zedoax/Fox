About Me

Hello, my name is Elijah Bendinsky. I am a current student at Rochester Institute of Technology. I am pursuing a major in Computer Science, with a minor in Computer Security and Japanese. My hobbies include camping, swimming, volunteering, computer research and repair, and reading japanese manga in japanese. One of my favorite accomplishments is the server network I privately own, created from scratch, and manage on my own. I host all of my media, files, websites, virtual machines, parent's television/dvr, and much more on said network. I am a member of RIT's Computer Science House, which is an organization founded for the goal of creating a community that allows members to work on projects to test and advance the boundaries of the Computer Science Field. I have found being a member of this organization to be one of my most enriching experiences. Many of my college friends are heavily involved in this organization, and help to further it's healthy and fun aptmosphere. My biggest dream is to start a successful digital manga service that licenses manga directly from Japan, so customers can get real time updates legally, without having to wait the absurd times for translations to reach the United States and other territories.
